The Format

How Debate Duel Works

01
Topic Generated

An Intelligent Contract fetches a fresh, thought-provoking topic — technology, environment, society — never the same debate twice. Both players are assigned sides at random.

02
Arguments Submitted

Player A argues FOR. Player B argues AGAINST. Each writes their argument — no templates, no hints. Pure reasoning under the clock.

03
AI Judges On-Chain

The LLM reads both arguments and scores each on Logic, Evidence, and Persuasion. The verdict and reasoning are written permanently to the GenLayer blockchain.

The Criteria

How You Win

🧠
Logic

Is your reasoning coherent? Does your argument hold up structurally? The AI tests whether your case actually leads to your conclusion.

Max 10 pts
📊
Evidence

Do you support your claims? Concrete examples, facts, analogies, and real-world references score higher than vague assertions.

Max 10 pts
🔥
Persuasion

Is your argument compelling? Clarity, structure, and rhetorical power matter. The AI judges how effectively you communicate your case.

Max 10 pts
